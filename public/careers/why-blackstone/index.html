Why Blackstone

At Blackstone, we don’t just think about recruitment; we invest in retention and development. Our objective is to recognize talent, respect hard work and reward success.

Learn More

Our employees are integral to the firm’s culture of integrity, professionalism and excellence.

At Blackstone, we look to attract and retain the brightest minds in the business, hiring professionals and students across a wide range of disciplines. Our employees’ passion for their work and commitment to their clients have helped Blackstone become one of the most respected financial institutions and a trusted partner for some of the largest investors in the world.

Our Most Valuable Asset

Our people are our most valuable asset. They are highly skilled problem solvers who believe challenges represent opportunities. Together with the firm’s entrepreneurial spirit, their unique passions and backgrounds contribute to a diverse, creative and vibrant environment.

The Employer of Choice

We encourage independent thinking and reward initiative, while providing new skills, training and mentoring to help our people grow professionally. Our global business platform and brand reputation provide an unparalleled launchpad for your growing career.

Diversity and Inclusion

At Blackstone, we actively promote diversity and inclusion inside our firm and throughout our industry. Our efforts include internal networks to engage our diverse populations; targeted recruiting efforts to attract qualified, diverse talent to Blackstone; and partnerships with external diversity- and inclusion-focused organizations.

Phishing Email Alert

Blackstone has recently become aware of fraudulent emails purporting to offer employment at Blackstone and misusing the official Blackstone logo. These emails do not originate from Blackstone or any of our affiliates. If you have received any such email, you should not click any links in the email or provide any personal information (e.g., your social security number or scans of identity documents).
