Students

Blackstone seeks to hire the best and brightest students into our Investment and Corporate businesses. Each line of business offers unique opportunities to grow and develop junior talent.

Learn More

Full-Time Program

Students in their last year of undergraduate studies or in their second year of business school with an anticipated graduation date in the spring may apply for our full-time Analyst and Associate positions.

Timeline

Our campus positions appear on the Blackstone website in June, while recruiting events take place on campuses across the globe. Interviews are conducted from June to September. First-round interviews can occur virtually or in our offices. Candidates selected for additional interviews will be invited to our offices for face-to-face meetings. In order to be considered for full-time opportunities, you must apply through the Blackstone website.

If we visit your school for an event, we encourage you to attend and network with our professionals to learn more about our opportunities. All other interested candidates can submit an application online. For open positions, please click here.

Summer Internship Program

Blackstone wants to be the employer of choice for the best young talent. Our internship program gives Summer Analysts and Associates an unprecedented opportunity to play an integral role in all aspects of deals, transactions and other projects.

Our summer program typically lasts 10–12 weeks. Students in their junior year of college, third year of university, or first year of business school may apply for our Summer Analyst and Associate positions. Interested candidates may apply to multiple groups within Blackstone.

Timeline

Our campus positions will appear on the Blackstone website in June, while recruiting events take place on campuses across the globe. Interviews are ongoing in our international offices based on hiring needs.

Summer intern hiring concludes by the end of October.

Applications for European summer internships are due in late November.

OVERVIEW
CREDIT

Blackstone Credit is one of the world’s largest credit-oriented asset managers. Our activities range from investing in public debt that offers attractive risk-adjusted returns to providing private capital to enable companies to remain viable. The solutions we offer are often not available elsewhere in the credit markets, and draw upon a range of non-investment grade disciplines, including leveraged loans, high yield bonds, structured credit, mezzanine lending, and rescue financing.

LEARN MORE ABOUT BLACKSTONE CREDIT

EXTERNAL RELATIONS

The External Relations group sets the strategy for shareholder, media, and government relations and the Blackstone Charitable Foundation. We communicate with constituents including shareholders, research analysts who cover Blackstone, the media, government officials, and not-for-profit organizations around the world. We are responsible for the firm’s social media presence, website, internal communications, and all aspects of the firm’s brand. We also run the Blackstone Charitable Foundation, which is focused on job creation and entrepreneurship and also facilitates employee volunteer activities globally.

FINANCE

The Blackstone Finance team provides analyses for the firm’s senior leaders to assist them in making strategic, timely business decisions, in addition to maintaining the books and records of the firm and its funds and financial controls. We manage the firm’s liquidity play an instrumental role in maximizing returns on the firm’s investments while minimizing risk. We also provide essential financial information to all investors. We are dedicated to leveraging and protecting the firm’s balance sheet to the benefit of our investors, clients, shareholders, and employees.

Frequently Asked Questions

Blackstone does not recruit at my university; am I still eligible to apply?

Blackstone considers candidates from schools across the globe. We encourage candidates to submit an application online. Candidates eligible for full-time opportunities are completing their final year of college or graduate school. Candidates eligible for summer opportunities are completing their third year of university or their junior year in college.

Do I need to submit a cover letter?

Cover letters are not necessary for campus applications.

I missed the application deadline; am I still eligible to apply?

No. Unfortunately, due to the high volume of applications we are unable to accommodate late submissions.

Are international students eligible to apply?

Yes. If you are not a citizen, Blackstone may petition on your behalf for an employment-based visa. Please be advised that the country’s citizen and immigration service office makes the ultimate determination as to whether such a visa will be granted.

Does Blackstone offer informational interviews?

Unfortunately, due to the small size of our firm we are unable to coordinate informational interviews or calls. Please check our website for additional information on our business groups.

I have just graduated. May I apply now?

For recent graduates, please visit our careers website for entry level positions. Our university programs are specifically for students still in undergraduate and/or graduate programs.

Do you offer unpaid internships?

No. Blackstone is unable to offer unpaid internships.

Phishing Email Alert

Blackstone has recently become aware of fraudulent emails purporting to offer employment at Blackstone and misusing the official Blackstone logo. These emails do not originate from Blackstone or any of our affiliates. If you have received any such email, you should not click any links in the email or provide any personal information (e.g., your social security number or scans of identity documents).
